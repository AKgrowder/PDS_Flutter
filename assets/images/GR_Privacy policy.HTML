  1. COOKIES

    Packaging Depot store uses "cookies" to keep track of the User's visits to the Sites and to store specific information about the User. A "cookie" is a small file that is sent to a user's browser and saved on their computer or other device. If User does not disable or remove the cookie, our services will be notified every time User uses the same device to access the Platform, and Packaging Depot store may then be aware of User visits and the User's usage patterns.

    In general, Packaging Depot store uses cookies to recognize Users and make it possible for Packaging Depot store to: i) access Users' Registration Information or Account Information so Users don't have to re-enter it; ii) collect statistical data about Users' usage; iii) research visiting patterns and help target advertisements based on Users' interests; iv) help Packaging Depot store's partners track Users' visits to the Platform and process orders; and v monitor progress and participation on the Platform.

    The browser that is installed on the user's device can be configured to control whether and how cookies are accepted. User has the option to modify those configurations. If the user selects the cookie-disabling function and rejects all cookies, they might have to enter information on the platform more frequently and some features might not work.
